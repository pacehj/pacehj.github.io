About Me

Hi, my name is Jehlani and I am a Designer located in Chicago, IL. I've always loved solving problems and implementing concepts to make users' lives easier. My interest in UX/UI Design came from my time studying Architecture at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. When I discovered there was a career path that dealt with empathizing with users, in the tech space, I began to research more about UX/UI design and I knew it was for me. Aside from my interest in User Experience and User Interface Design, I also enjoy:

  • Watching Sci-Fi films
  • Lifting weights
  • Playing the piano
